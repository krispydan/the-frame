## The brand

Jaxy started with 30 years of eyewear experience, a clean slate, and one idea: great frames shouldn't cost a fortune. An independent label born in Los Angeles — bold shapes, sun-drenched colors, a little retro energy. Every detail intentional, every price sensible.

**Website:** getjaxy.com
**Name:** always *Jaxy*, never *Jaxy Eyewear* — don't append the category to the wordmark.

### Voice
Approachable and warm, but never vague. Confident, never arrogant. Honest. No jargon, no empty superlatives, no corporate filler — clear, human language.

- Tone: approachable, confident, honest, modern, warm
- Language: simple, human, direct; no exaggeration

Example lines: *"Good glasses. No pressure." · "Style, minus the stress." · "Wear them. Live in them."*

## Logo

The Jaxy logo is a **custom wordmark** (chunky retro display face, *Glitz*). The wordmark is the logo.

### Versions
- **Primary logo** — the full `Jaxy` wordmark. Use wherever the brand needs to be clearly recognized.
- **Secondary version** — the `x` icon (swash mark). Use where the name is already present and just needs reinforcement: app icons, social avatars, repeated mentions.

### Minimum size
- **Primary wordmark:** 0.5 in / 36 px wide
- **Secondary `x` icon:** 0.19 in / 13 px wide

### Clear space
Use the secondary `x` as the reference unit — keep that same distance clear on all four sides. Nothing crosses it.

### Don'ts
Don't stretch or distort · don't add glow · don't add an extra outline · don't reflect/flip · don't add a gradient · don't use unapproved colors · don't change the official colors · don't place over images that hurt readability · don't recreate it in a look-alike font (use the file).

## Shape system

A set of soft, organic "lens" shapes (rounded blobs, droplets, soft rectangles) used as decorative-but-meaningful elements to frame content and highlight details across ads and presentations. Pair them in brand colors (e.g. lavender + plum, sage + moss). They're an extension of the identity, not just decoration.
