## Why split_50_50 hero works for Faire event

The split variant puts the product/lifestyle image on the LEFT
half and HTML text overlay on the RIGHT half — visually mirrors
how Faire's own storefront layout works (image left, text right
on every product card). When the buyer clicks through, the email
visual matches the destination — small but real continuity boost.

## The deliberate restraint

The body never says "DON'T MISS!" or "LAST CHANCE!" — both banned.
Doesn't use a deadline-anxious tone even though there IS a deadline.
Just states the facts: "Through June 25." Lets the math do the
selling. The brand bible's worldview ("never anxious about our
prices") shows up here as "never anxious about the deadline either."
